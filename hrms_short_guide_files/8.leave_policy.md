# Leave Policy

## Overview
Leave Policy DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `amended_from` (Link, Read-only, Options: Leave Policy): Amended From
- `title` (Data, Required): Title

### Child Tables

- `leave_policy_details` (Table): Leave Policy Details
  - Options: Leave Policy Detail

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Leave Policy → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `title`: Title

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
