# Employee Onboarding

## Overview
Employee Onboarding DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `job_applicant` (Link, Required, Options: Job Applicant): Job Applicant
- `job_offer` (Link, Required, Options: Job Offer): Job Offer
- `employee_name` (Data, Required): Employee Name
- `employee` (Link, Read-only, Options: Employee): Employee
- `date_of_joining` (Date, Required): Date of Joining
- `boarding_status` (Select, Read-only, Options: Pending
In Process
Completed, Default: Pending): Status
- `notify_users_by_email` (Check, Default: 0): Notify users by email
- `employee_onboarding_template` (Link, Options: Employee Onboarding Template): Employee Onboarding Template
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company
- `department` (Link, Options: Department): Department
- `designation` (Link, Options: Designation): Designation
- `employee_grade` (Link, Options: Employee Grade): Employee Grade
- `project` (Link, Read-only, Options: Project): Project
- `amended_from` (Link, Read-only, Options: Employee Onboarding): Amended From
- `boarding_begins_on` (Date, Required): Onboarding Begins On
- `holiday_list` (Link, Options: Holiday List): Holiday List

### Child Tables

- `activities` (Table): Activities
  - Options: Employee Boarding Activity

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Employee Onboarding → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `job_applicant`: Job Applicant
   - `job_offer`: Job Offer
   - `employee_name`: Employee Name
   - `date_of_joining`: Date of Joining
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
