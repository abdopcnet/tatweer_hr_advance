# Job Opening

## Overview
Job Opening DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `job_title` (Data, Required): Job Title
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company
- `status` (Select, Options: Open
Closed): Status
- `designation` (Link, Required, Options: Designation): Designation
- `department` (Link, Options: Department): Department
- `publish` (Check, Default: 0): Publish on website
- `route` (Data): Route (Depends on: publish)
- `description` (Text Editor): Description
- `currency` (Link, Options: Currency): Currency
- `lower_range` (Currency): Lower Range
- `upper_range` (Currency): Upper Range
- `job_application_route` (Data): Job Application Route (Depends on: publish)
- `publish_salary_range` (Check, Default: 0): Publish Salary Range
- `posted_on` (Datetime, Default: Now): Posted On
- `closes_on` (Date): Closes On (Depends on: eval:doc.status == 'Open')

*... and 6 more fields*

### Read-only Fields

- `staffing_plan` (Link, Read-only, Options: Staffing Plan): Staffing Plan
- `planned_vacancies` (Int, Read-only): Planned number of Positions (Depends on: staffing_plan)
- `job_requisition` (Link, Read-only, Options: Job Requisition): Job Requisition
- `vacancies` (Int, Read-only): Vacancies (Fetched from: job_requisition.no_of_positions) (Depends on: job_requisition)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Job Opening → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `job_title`: Job Title
   - `company`: Company
   - `designation`: Designation

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-OPN-.YYYY.-.####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
