# Expense Claim

## Overview
Expense Claim DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `naming_series` (Select, Required, Options: HR-EXP-.YYYY.-): Series
- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): From Employee
- `department` (Link, Options: Department): Department (Fetched from: employee.department)
- `expense_approver` (Link, Options: User): Expense Approver
- `approval_status` (Select, Options: Draft
Approved
Rejected, Default: Draft): Approval Status
- `is_paid` (Check, Default: 0): Is Paid (Depends on: eval:(doc.docstatus==0 || doc.is_paid))
- `posting_date` (Date, Required): Posting Date
- `project` (Link, Options: Project): Project
- `task` (Link, Options: Task): Task
- `remark` (Small Text): Remark
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company (Fetched from: employee.company)
- `mode_of_payment` (Link, Options: Mode of Payment): Mode of Payment (Depends on: is_paid)
- `clearance_date` (Date): Clearance Date
- `payable_account` (Link, Options: Account): Payable Account (Fetched from: company.default_expense_claim_payable_account)
- `cost_center` (Link, Options: Cost Center): Cost Center (Fetched from: company.cost_center)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Expense Claim → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `naming_series`: Series
   - `employee`: From Employee
   - `posting_date`: Posting Date
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document
