# Income Tax Slab

## Overview
Income Tax Slab DocType for HRMS Payroll.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `effective_from` (Date, Required): Effective from
- `allow_tax_exemption` (Check, Default: 0): Allow Tax Exemption
- `disabled` (Check, Default: 0): Disabled
- `standard_tax_exemption_amount` (Currency): Standard Tax Exemption Amount
- `company` (Link, Options: Company): Company
- `currency` (Link, Required, Options: Currency): Currency (Fetched from: company.default_currency)
- `tax_relief_limit` (Currency): Taxable Income Relief Threshold Limit

### Child Tables

- `slabs` (Table): Taxable Salary Slabs
  - Options: Taxable Salary Slab
- `other_taxes_and_charges` (Table): Other Taxes and Charges
  - Options: Income Tax Slab Other Charges

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS Payroll → Income Tax Slab → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `effective_from`: Effective from
   - `currency`: Currency

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: Prompt

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
