# Interview

## Overview
Interview DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `job_applicant` (Link, Required, Options: Job Applicant): Job Applicant
- `interview_round` (Link, Required, Options: Interview Round): Interview Round
- `status` (Select, Required, Options: Pending
Under Review
Cleared
Rejected, Default: Pending): Status
- `interview_summary` (Text): interview_summary
- `resume_link` (Data): Resume link (Fetched from: job_applicant.resume_link)
- `scheduled_on` (Date, Required): Scheduled On
- `reminded` (Check, Default: 0): Reminded
- `from_time` (Time, Required): From Time
- `to_time` (Time, Required): To Time

### Read-only Fields

- `job_opening` (Link, Read-only, Options: Job Opening): Job Opening (Fetched from: job_applicant.job_title)
- `average_rating` (Rating, Read-only): Obtained Average Rating
- `expected_average_rating` (Rating, Read-only): Expected Average Rating (Fetched from: interview_round.expected_average_rating)
- `designation` (Link, Read-only, Options: Designation): Designation (Fetched from: interview_round.designation)

### Child Tables

- `interview_details` (Table): Interviewers
  - Options: Interview Detail

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Interview → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `job_applicant`: Job Applicant
   - `interview_round`: Interview Round
   - `status`: Status
   - `scheduled_on`: Scheduled On
   - `from_time`: From Time
   - `to_time`: To Time

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-INT-.YYYY.-.####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
