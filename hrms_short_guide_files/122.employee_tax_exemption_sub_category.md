# Employee Tax Exemption Sub Category

## Overview
Employee Tax Exemption Sub Category DocType for HRMS Payroll.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `exemption_category` (Link, Required, Options: Employee Tax Exemption Category): Tax Exemption Category
- `max_amount` (Currency): Max Exemption Amount (Fetched from: exemption_category.max_amount)
- `is_active` (Check, Default: 1): Is Active

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS Payroll → Employee Tax Exemption Sub Category → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `exemption_category`: Tax Exemption Category

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
