# Job Offer

## Overview
Job Offer DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `job_applicant` (Link, Required, Options: Job Applicant): Job Applicant
- `status` (Select, Options: Awaiting Response
Accepted
Rejected): Status
- `offer_date` (Date, Required): Offer Date
- `designation` (Link, Required, Options: Designation): Designation (Fetched from: job_applicant.designation)
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company
- `select_terms` (Link, Options: Terms and Conditions): Select Terms and Conditions
- `terms` (Text Editor): Terms and Conditions
- `letter_head` (Link, Options: Letter Head): Letter Head (Fetched from: company.default_letter_head)
- `select_print_heading` (Link, Options: Print Heading): Print Heading
- `job_offer_term_template` (Link, Options: Job Offer Term Template): Job Offer Term Template

### Read-only Fields

- `applicant_name` (Data, Required, Read-only): Applicant Name (Fetched from: job_applicant.applicant_name)
- `applicant_email` (Data, Read-only): Applicant Email Address (Fetched from: job_applicant.email_id)

### Child Tables

- `offer_terms` (Table): Job Offer Terms
  - Options: Job Offer Term

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Job Offer → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `job_applicant`: Job Applicant
   - `offer_date`: Offer Date
   - `designation`: Designation
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-OFF-.YYYY.-.#####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
