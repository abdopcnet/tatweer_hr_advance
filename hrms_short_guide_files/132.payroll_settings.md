# Payroll Settings

## Overview
Payroll Settings DocType for HRMS Payroll.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `payroll_based_on` (Select, Options: Leave
Attendance, Default: Leave): Calculate Payroll Working Days Based On
- `max_working_hours_against_timesheet` (Float): Max working hours against Timesheet
- `include_holidays_in_total_working_days` (Check, Default: 0): Include holidays in Total no. of Working Days
- `disable_rounded_total` (Check, Default: 0): Disable Rounded Total
- `daily_wages_fraction_for_half_day` (Float, Default: 0.5): Fraction of Daily Salary for Half Day
- `email_salary_slip_to_employee` (Check, Default: 1): Email Salary Slip to Employee
- `encrypt_salary_slips_in_emails` (Check, Default: 0): Encrypt Salary Slips in Emails (Depends on: eval: doc.email_salary_slip_to_employee == 1;)
- `password_policy` (Data): Password Policy (Depends on: eval: doc.encrypt_salary_slips_in_emails == 1)
- `consider_unmarked_attendance_as` (Select, Options: Present
Absent): Consider Unmarked Attendance As (Depends on: eval:doc.payroll_based_on == 'Attendance')
- `show_leave_balances_in_salary_slip` (Check, Default: 0): Show Leave Balances in Salary Slip
- `process_payroll_accounting_entry_based_on_employee` (Check, Default: 0): Process Payroll Accounting Entry based on Employee
- `consider_marked_attendance_on_holidays` (Check, Default: 0): Consider Marked Attendance on Holidays (Depends on: include_holidays_in_total_working_days)
- `sender` (Link, Options: Email Account): Sender (Depends on: eval:doc.email_salary_slip_to_employee)
- `email_template` (Link, Options: Email Template): Email Template (Depends on: eval:doc.email_salary_slip_to_employee)

### Read-only Fields

- `sender_email` (Data, Read-only): Sender Email (Fetched from: sender.email_id) (Depends on: eval:doc.sender)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS Payroll → Payroll Settings → New

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
