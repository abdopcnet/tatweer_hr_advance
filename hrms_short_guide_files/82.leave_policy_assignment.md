# Leave Policy Assignment

## Overview
Leave Policy Assignment DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): Employee
- `leave_policy` (Link, Required, Options: Leave Policy): Leave Policy
- `assignment_based_on` (Select, Options: 
Leave Period
Joining Date): Assignment based on
- `leave_period` (Link, Options: Leave Period): Leave Period (Depends on: eval:doc.assignment_based_on == "Leave Period")
- `effective_from` (Date, Required): Effective From
- `effective_to` (Date, Required): Effective To
- `carry_forward` (Check, Default: 0): Add unused leaves from previous allocations
- `leaves_allocated` (Check, Default: 0): Leaves Allocated

### Read-only Fields

- `employee_name` (Data, Read-only): Employee name (Fetched from: employee.employee_name)
- `company` (Link, Read-only, Options: Company): Company (Fetched from: employee.company)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Leave Policy Assignment → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `employee`: Employee
   - `leave_policy`: Leave Policy
   - `effective_from`: Effective From
   - `effective_to`: Effective To

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-LPOL-ASSGN-.#####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
