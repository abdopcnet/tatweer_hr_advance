# Leave Allocation

## Overview
Leave Allocation DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `naming_series` (Select, Required, Options: HR-LAL-.YYYY.-): Series
- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): Employee
- `employee_name` (Data, Read-only): Employee Name
- `department` (Link, Read-only, Options: Department): Department
- `leave_type` (Link, Required, Options: Leave Type): Leave Type
- `from_date` (Date, Required): From Date
- `to_date` (Date, Required): To Date
- `new_leaves_allocated` (Float): New Leaves Allocated
- `carry_forward` (Check, Default: 0): Add unused leaves from previous allocations
- `unused_leaves` (Float, Read-only): Unused leaves
- `total_leaves_allocated` (Float, Required, Read-only): Total Leaves Allocated
- `total_leaves_encashed` (Float, Read-only): Total Leaves Encashed
- `compensatory_request` (Link, Read-only, Options: Compensatory Leave Request): Compensatory Leave Request
- `leave_period` (Link, Read-only, Options: Leave Period): Leave Period
- `leave_policy` (Link, Read-only, Options: Leave Policy): Leave Policy
- `expired` (Check, Read-only, Default: 0): Expired
- `amended_from` (Link, Read-only, Options: Leave Allocation): Amended From
- `description` (Small Text): Description
- `carry_forwarded_leaves_count` (Float, Read-only): Carry Forwarded Leaves
- `leave_policy_assignment` (Link, Read-only, Options: Leave Policy Assignment): Leave Policy Assignment
- `company` (Link, Required, Read-only, Options: Company): Company

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Leave Allocation → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `naming_series`: Series
   - `employee`: Employee
   - `leave_type`: Leave Type
   - `from_date`: From Date
   - `to_date`: To Date

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
