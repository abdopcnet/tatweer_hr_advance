# Employee Benefit Claim

## Overview
Employee Benefit Claim DocType for HRMS Payroll.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): Employee
- `claim_date` (Date, Required): Claim Date
- `earning_component` (Link, Required, Options: Salary Component): Claim Benefit For
- `claimed_amount` (Currency, Required): Claimed Amount
- `attachments` (Attach): Attachments
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company (Fetched from: employee.company)

### Read-only Fields

- `employee_name` (Data, Read-only): Employee Name (Fetched from: employee.employee_name)
- `department` (Link, Read-only, Options: Department): Department (Fetched from: employee.department)
- `max_amount_eligible` (Currency, Read-only): Max Amount Eligible (Fetched from: earning_component.max_benefit_amount)
- `pay_against_benefit_claim` (Check, Read-only, Default: 0): Pay Against Benefit Claim (Fetched from: earning_component.pay_against_benefit_claim)
- `salary_slip` (Link, Read-only, Options: Salary Slip): Salary Slip
- `currency` (Link, Required, Read-only, Options: Currency): Currency (Depends on: eval: doc.employee)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS Payroll → Employee Benefit Claim → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `employee`: Employee
   - `claim_date`: Claim Date
   - `earning_component`: Claim Benefit For
   - `claimed_amount`: Claimed Amount
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-BEN-CLM-.YY.-.MM.-.#####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
