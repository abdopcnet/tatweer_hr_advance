# Full and Final Statement

## Overview
Full and Final Statement DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): Employee
- `transaction_date` (Date, Required): Transaction Date

### Read-only Fields

- `employee_name` (Data, Read-only): Employee Name (Fetched from: employee.employee_name)
- `designation` (Link, Read-only, Options: Designation): Designation (Fetched from: employee.designation)
- `status` (Select, Read-only, Options: Paid
Unpaid, Default: Unpaid): Status
- `department` (Link, Read-only, Options: Department): Department (Fetched from: employee.department)
- `relieving_date` (Date, Read-only): Relieving Date  (Fetched from: employee.relieving_date)
- `date_of_joining` (Date, Read-only): Date of Joining (Fetched from: employee.date_of_joining)
- `company` (Link, Read-only, Options: Company): Company (Fetched from: employee.company)
- `total_payable_amount` (Currency, Read-only): Total Payable Amount
- `total_receivable_amount` (Currency, Read-only): Total Receivable Amount
- `total_asset_recovery_cost` (Currency, Read-only): Total Asset Recovery Cost

### Child Tables

- `assets_allocated` (Table): assets_allocated
  - Options: Full and Final Asset
- `payables` (Table): payables
  - Options: Full and Final Outstanding Statement
- `receivables` (Table): receivables
  - Options: Full and Final Outstanding Statement

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Full and Final Statement → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `employee`: Employee
   - `transaction_date`: Transaction Date

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-FNF-.YYYY.-.#####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
