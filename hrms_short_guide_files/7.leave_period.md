# Leave Period

## Overview
Leave Period DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `from_date` (Date, Required): From Date
- `to_date` (Date, Required): To Date
- `is_active` (Check, Default: 0): Is Active
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company
- `optional_holiday_list` (Link, Options: Holiday List): Holiday List for Optional Leave

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Leave Period → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `from_date`: From Date
   - `to_date`: To Date
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
