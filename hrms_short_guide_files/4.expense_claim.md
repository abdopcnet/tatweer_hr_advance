# Expense Claim

## Overview
Expense Claim DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `naming_series` (Select, Required, Options: HR-EXP-.YYYY.-): Series
- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): From Employee
- `employee_name` (Data, Read-only): Employee Name
- `department` (Link, Options: Department): Department
- `expense_approver` (Link, Options: User): Expense Approver
- `approval_status` (Select, Options: Draft
Approved
Rejected, Default: Draft): Approval Status
- `total_claimed_amount` (Currency, Read-only, Options: Company:company:default_currency): Total Claimed Amount
- `total_sanctioned_amount` (Currency, Read-only, Options: Company:company:default_currency): Total Sanctioned Amount
- `is_paid` (Check, Default: 0): Is Paid
- `posting_date` (Date, Required, Default: Today): Posting Date
- `vehicle_log` (Link, Read-only, Options: Vehicle Log): Vehicle Log
- `project` (Link, Options: Project): Project
- `task` (Link, Options: Task): Task
- `total_amount_reimbursed` (Currency, Read-only, Options: Company:company:default_currency): Total Amount Reimbursed
- `remark` (Small Text): Remark
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company
- `mode_of_payment` (Link, Options: Mode of Payment): Mode of Payment
- `clearance_date` (Date): Clearance Date
- `payable_account` (Link, Options: Account): Payable Account
- `cost_center` (Link, Options: Cost Center): Cost Center
- `status` (Select, Read-only, Options: Draft
Paid
Unpaid
Rejected
Submitted
Cancelled, Default: Draft): Status
- `amended_from` (Link, Read-only, Options: Expense Claim): Amended From
- `total_advance_amount` (Currency, Read-only, Options: Company:company:default_currency): Total Advance Amount
- `grand_total` (Currency, Read-only, Options: Company:company:default_currency): Grand Total
- `total_taxes_and_charges` (Currency, Read-only, Options: Company:company:default_currency): Total Taxes and Charges
- `delivery_trip` (Link, Options: Delivery Trip): Delivery Trip

### Child Tables

- `expenses` (Table): Expenses
  - Options: Expense Claim Detail
- `advances` (Table): Advances
  - Options: Expense Claim Advance
- `taxes` (Table): Expense Taxes and Charges
  - Options: Expense Taxes and Charges

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Expense Claim → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `naming_series`: Series
   - `employee`: From Employee
   - `posting_date`: Posting Date
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
