# Employee Incentive

## Overview
Employee Incentive DocType for HRMS Payroll.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `employee` (Link, Required, Options: Employee): Employee
- `incentive_amount` (Currency, Required): Incentive Amount
- `payroll_date` (Date, Required): Payroll Date
- `salary_component` (Link, Required, Options: Salary Component): Salary Component
- `company` (Link, Required, Options: Company): Company

### Read-only Fields

- `employee_name` (Data, Read-only): Employee Name (Fetched from: employee.employee_name)
- `department` (Link, Read-only, Options: Department): Department (Fetched from: employee.department)
- `currency` (Link, Required, Read-only, Options: Currency): Currency (Depends on: eval:(doc.docstatus==1 || doc.employee))

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS Payroll → Employee Incentive → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `employee`: Employee
   - `incentive_amount`: Incentive Amount
   - `payroll_date`: Payroll Date
   - `salary_component`: Salary Component
   - `company`: Company

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document
3. Submit document

### Naming
- Auto-naming: HR-EINV-.YY.-.MM.-.#####

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
- Document must be submitted to be effective
