# Upload Attendance

## Overview
Upload Attendance DocType for HRMS.

## Fields

### Main Fields

- `att_fr_date` (Date, Required): Attendance From Date
- `att_to_date` (Date, Required): Attendance To Date

## Workflow

### Step 1: Create Document
1. Go to HRMS → Upload Attendance → New
2. Fill required fields:
   - `att_fr_date`: Attendance From Date
   - `att_to_date`: Attendance To Date

### Step 2: Save/Submit
1. Review all fields
2. Save document

## Important Notes
- Document must be saved to be effective
